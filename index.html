Thin Lens

Thin lens camera is the alternative camera of perspective camera. It has the adjustable aperture radius and focal distance, which enables depth of field effects and enhances the realism of our scene.
The aperture radius is the size of the lens opening which controls how much light enters. A larger aperture results in a shallower depth of field and more blur in out-of-focus areas.
The focal distance descibes at which distance the image is sharp. It defines the focus point with objects closer or farther from it appearing increasingly blurred.

Roughness alpha = 0.01

Halton Sampler

Halton Sampler is a better random number generator to reduce noise. It allows to generate low-discrepancy sample points for an arbitrary number of integral dimensions. This generates a sequence of points that are much more evenly distributed than those produced by the independent sampler.
